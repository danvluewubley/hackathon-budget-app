Budgeting Made Simple (BMS) is a user-friendly budgeting app designed specifically for first-generation families in New York to manage their finances effectively. BMS has budgeting features giving the individual a plan to handle their spendings. Users can customize the percentages given to each category, such as retirement, living expenses, savings, and more! BMS has a tax calculator that gives a rough estimate of their remaining income after tax. The tax calculations we created are more accurate for families with an income less than $100,000 per year in New York.
